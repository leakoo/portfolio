ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Dan, an aspiring frontend developer passionate about building web experiences from the ground up with clean, visually appealing designs. I enjoy learning new technologies to sharpen my skills, with a strong focus on React and modern JavaScript. I regularly work on personal projects to improve my portfolio, explore animation tools like GSAP, and continue expanding into new technologies like TypeScript.

I’ve mainly worked solo, so I’m excited and curious about contributing to a team and growing through real-world development work. I love creating projects I’m passionate about, especially ones that let me explore new ideas. My goal is to keep improving, refining my skills, and eventually land a role where I can contribute and learn from others.
